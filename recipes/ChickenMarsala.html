Chicken Marsala

Back to Homepage

Description

Will's favorite meal!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Mushrooms
  • Shallots
  • Marsala
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Heavy Cream
  • Chicken Stock

Steps

  1. Brown the chicken and set aside
  2. Saute shallots and garlic until fragrant
  3. Deglaze pan with marsala wine
  4. Add cream and chicken stock and reduce until slightly thickened
  5. Finish chicken in sauce and scrape juices left on plate into sauce
