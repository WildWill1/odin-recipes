Hot Cocoa

Back to Homepage

Description

Warm hot cocoa on a winter's night

My sister's favorite drink.

Ingredients

  • chocolate
  • milk
  • marshmallows
  • whipped cream

Steps

  1. Mix 4 cups of whole milk with 0.5 cups of cocoa powder until powder has dissolved
  2. Heat in microwave on high for 4 minutes
  3. Squirt whipped cream over the top
  4. Enjoy!
